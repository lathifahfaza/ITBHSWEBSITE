INSTITUTE TECHNOLOGY BLOXY HIGH SCHOOL (ITBHS) RULES AND REGULATIONS



1. Attendance and Punctuality


Mandatory Attendance: All students must attend classes regularly. A minimum attendance of 90% per semester is required unless excused for valid reasons.
Timeliness: Students must arrive on time for all classes and school activities. Late arrivals disrupt the learning process and may result in disciplinary action.
Leave of Absence: In case of illness or emergencies, students must submit a formal leave request signed by a parent or guardian.

2. Dress Code


Uniform Policy: Students must wear the prescribed school uniform at all times unless instructed otherwise for special events. The uniform must be neat and presentable.
Grooming Standards: Personal grooming should reflect the school’s standards of cleanliness and respect. Extreme hairstyles, excessive jewelry, and inappropriate makeup are prohibited.
Footwear: Only closed-toe shoes are allowed for safety and uniformity purposes.

3. Behavior and Conduct


Respect: Students must show respect to all faculty, staff, peers, and visitors at ITBHS. Disrespectful behavior, including bullying, harassment, or inappropriate language, will not be tolerated.
Classroom Etiquette: During class, students are expected to participate actively, listen attentively, and refrain from using electronic devices unless instructed by the teacher for academic purposes.
Public Spaces: While on school premises, students must maintain cleanliness, keep noise levels appropriate, and avoid engaging in disruptive behavior..

4. Academic Integrity


Honesty: Students must wear the prescribed school uniform at all times unless instructed otherwise for special events. The uniform must be neat and presentable.
Grooming Standards: Personal grooming should reflect the school’s standards of cleanliness and respect. Extreme hairstyles, excessive jewelry, and inappropriate makeup are prohibited.
Footwear: Only closed-toe shoes are allowed for safety and uniformity purposes.

5. Use of Technology


School Devices: School-provided devices such as computers and tablets must be used for educational purposes only. Personal use, including gaming and social media browsing, is not permitted during school hours.
Mobile Phones: Mobile phones must be switched off or set to silent mode during class hours and kept in lockers. Unauthorized use of phones during lessons will result in confiscation.
Cyber Conduct: All students must maintain respectful behavior online. Cyberbullying or misuse of school networks will lead to strict consequences.

6. Extracurricular Activities


Participation: Students are encouraged to participate in school clubs, sports, and extra-curricular activities. Commitment to these activities should not interfere with academic responsibilities.
Behavior in Events: During school events, students must conduct themselves appropriately, representing ITBHS with pride and integrity both on and off campus.

7. Property and Environtment


School Property: Students must take care of all school property, including books, equipment, and furniture. Damages caused intentionally or through negligence will be the responsibility of the student.
Personal Belongings: Students are responsible for their personal belongings. ITBHS is not liable for the loss or damage of personal items.
Cleanliness: All students must contribute to keeping the school environment clean. Littering, defacing walls, or damaging school facilities will lead to disciplinary action.

8. Savety and Security


Emergency Prodecures: Students must familiarize themselves with school emergency procedures and adhere to instructions during drills or actual emergencies.
Restricted Areas: Students are not permitted to enter restricted areas such as staff rooms, laboratories without supervision, or other designated locations without permission.
Substance Abuse: The possession, use, or distribution of illegal substances, alcohol, or tobacco on school premises is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate expulsion.

9. Parental Involement


Communication: Parents are encouraged to maintain regular communication with the school regarding their child’s progress and behavior.
Meetings: Parents must attend scheduled parent-teacher meetings and respond to school communications in a timely manner.

10. Disciplinary Actions


Consequences: Failure to comply with the above rules will result in disciplinary measures, which may include detention, suspension, or expulsion, depending on the severity of the violation.
